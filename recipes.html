Kitchen Essentials

Tools & Ingredients for Success

Must-Have Kitchen Tools

  • Nonstick skillet or cast iron pan
  • Sharp chef's knife
  • Cutting boards
  • Blender or food processor
  • Sheet pan for roasting

Clean Eating Staples

  • Fresh leafy greens & veggies
  • Lean proteins (chicken, fish, tofu)
  • Whole grains like quinoa
  • Eggs & healthy fats

Pro Tips for Busy Cooks

  • Pre-chop veggies
  • Store healthy foods at eye level
  • Limit processed snacks
  • Keep frozen grains & veggies
