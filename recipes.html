Grilled Lemon Herb Chicken Salad

20 mins • 2 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon (zested & juiced)
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 6 cups salad greens
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta (optional)

Instructions

  1. Mix marinade ingredients & coat chicken.
  2. Grill chicken 6–7 mins per side.
  3. Assemble salad with greens & veggies.
  4. Slice chicken & place on salad.
  5. Top with feta, serve with lemon wedges.

Quinoa and Black Bean Bowl

25 mins • 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1 can black beans, rinsed
  • 1 cup corn
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1 tsp cumin

Instructions

  1. Cook quinoa per package directions.
  2. Mix quinoa, beans, corn, pepper, salsa, and cumin.
  3. Serve warm or cold, garnish with cilantro.

Zucchini Noodle Stir-Fry

15 mins • 2 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 zucchinis, spiralized
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil

Instructions

  1. Sauté veggies until tender.
  2. Add zoodles, soy sauce, sesame oil.
  3. Toss & cook 2–3 mins, serve hot.

Broiled Salmon with Garlic Asparagus

15 mins • 2 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1 bunch asparagus
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 lemon wedge

Instructions

  1. Preheat broiler, line sheet pan.
  2. Toss asparagus with olive oil & garlic.
  3. Place salmon & asparagus on pan.
  4. Broil 8–10 mins until salmon is flaky.
  5. Squeeze lemon over top & serve.

Chickpea and Veggie Power Wraps

10 mins • 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas, mashed
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup cucumber slices
  • Whole wheat wraps
  • 2 tbsp hummus

Instructions

  1. Spread hummus on wraps.
  2. Add mashed chickpeas & veggies.
  3. Roll tightly, slice in half & serve.

Greek Yogurt Berry Parfait

5 mins • 1 serving

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup fresh berries
  • 1 tbsp granola
  • 1 tsp honey

Instructions

  1. Layer yogurt, berries, and granola.
  2. Drizzle with honey & serve immediately.
